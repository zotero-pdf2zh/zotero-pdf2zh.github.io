# Translation Options

Right-click on an entry or PDF in Zotero and select "PDF2zh" translation options.

## Translation Options

### Translate PDF

Click on the original PDF or paper entry to generate the translated PDF.

### Crop PDF

Select dual/mono type attachment to crop at 1/2 width and splice vertically. Suitable for mobile reading.

### Compare PDF

Generate a PDF with original text on the left and translated text on the right.

### Crop-Compare PDF

For **double-column PDF papers**. First crops vertically to single-column, then splices left-right.
