## How to Ask Effectively

Before asking, please:

1. **Read documentation** - Check FAQ and terminal error messages
2. **Search existing issues** - Ensure it's not a known problem
3. **Provide information**:
   - Complete terminal logs
   - Zotero settings screenshot
   - Error popup screenshot
   - Description of attempted solutions
